# Platform Mesh

Defines an environment where **service providers offer services** and **service consumers discover, order capabilities, and control their lifecycle**.

Transcends the traditional IaaS/PaaS/SaaS layering -- both higher-level and lower-level services managed through the same mechanism.

## Key Technologies

- **kcp** - [User Facing Control Plane](User-Facing-Control-Plane.md) foundation (KRM-based API)
- **[OpenMFP](../5-software-delivery/OpenMFP.md)** - micro frontend integration
- **OpenFGA** - relationship-based authorization
- **Keycloak** - identity management
