# Data Fabric Components

Three main components:

## Open Resource Discovery (ORD)

Protocol providing a common standard for describing business applications. Exposes metadata for system capabilities (API, event, data product) and business objects via a standardized ORD endpoint. Open-source.

## Unified Metadata Service (UMS)

Central service that collects all ORD-based metadata of participating services. Aggregates metadata of a customer landscape and serves as a Discovery API. Enables automated understanding of how business objects relate across capabilities and applications.

## Aligned Metadata Model

Standardized metadata model ensuring consistent identification of business objects independent of resource or service naming conventions. Uses dedicated annotations to define the business object associated with an API.
