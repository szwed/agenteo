# Digital Twins

A **digital twin** is a virtual representation of a physical object, system, or process, continuously updated with real-time data to mirror its real-world counterpart.

Used across manufacturing, healthcare, urban planning, automotive to improve efficiency, predict failures, and enhance performance.

## Core Value

1. **Virtual representation** -- real-world data with desired and actual state
2. **Feedback loop** -- enables simulation, analysis, optimization, orchestration
3. **Actuators** -- controllers that close the loop, providing control over complex systems
